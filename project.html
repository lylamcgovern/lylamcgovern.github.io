The Journey of an Influencer

Role: Writer / Director / Editor

A short film about a young person who becomes consumed by the need for likes and shapes their identity around an imagined audience. The film examines the gaze—how the protagonist views themself through perceived online judgment—and follows their journey to reclaim authenticity.

  • Length: ~7–10 minutes
  • Tools: DSLR, Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe Audition, Figma for storyboards
  • Planned: Final color grade, festival submissions, portfolio screening
